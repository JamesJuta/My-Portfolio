Learn About My Artistic Background

"Behind the Scenes: My Journey in Art and Design"

I am a graphic designer with over two years of experience in the field of graphic design. When I was in high school, I was a member of the campus journalism club, where my role was to design newspapers to be used for division and regional competitions. I have been training and being exposed to design for almost 7 years with graphic design tools, mainly Adobe Photoshop and Indesign, that have become a stepping stone for me as a graphic designer.
Later, when I became more passionate about design, I practiced and studied more design tools and principles to improve my graphic design skills and give more value to the clients that trust me. Furthermore, I first became a freelancer when my brother was establishing his business, a mobile coffee shop, where I made all the related graphics needed for the business.
